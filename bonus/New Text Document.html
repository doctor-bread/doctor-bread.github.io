Math Substitution Guide

x = x

Remember, they always have to be equal.

Addition -> Subtract

x + 5 = 8

When numbers jump over the equals sign, they become the counterpart of what they are.
+5 jumps over and becomes −5.

x = 8 − 5 x = 3

Subtraction -> Add

x − 7 = 10

−7 jumps over the equals sign and becomes +7.

x = 10 + 7 x = 17

Multiplication -> Divide

4x = 20

4x means x × 4.
×4 jumps over the equals sign and becomes ÷4.

x = 20 ÷ 4 x = 5

Division -> Multiply

x ÷ 3 = 6

÷3 jumps over the equals sign and becomes ×3.

x = 6 × 3 x = 18

Combining Like Terms

x + x + 4 = 10 2x + 4 = 10

2x means x × 2.
+4 jumps over and becomes −4.

2x = 10 − 4 2x = 6 x = 6 ÷ 2 x = 3

Variables on Both Sides

3x + 2 = x + 10

3x means x × 3.
+x jumps left and becomes −x.
+2 jumps right and becomes −2.

3x + 2 = x + 10 3x - x + 2 = 10 3x - x = 10 - 2 3x − x = 10 − 2 2x = 8 x = 8 ÷ 2 x = 4

Brackets (Expand First)

2(x + 3) = 14

2(x + 3) means 2 × (x + 3).

2x + 6 = 14 2x = 14 − 6 2x = 8 x = 4

Fractions

x / 5 = 4

÷5 jumps over the equals sign and becomes ×5.

x = 4 × 5 x = 20

Squares -> Square Root

x² = 25

x² means x × x.
² jumps over and becomes √.

x = √25 x = ±5

Square Roots -> Square

√x = 4

√ jumps over and becomes ².

x = 4² x = 16

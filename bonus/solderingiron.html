Soldering Iron Safety Tips:

  1. Always work in a well-ventilated area to avoid inhaling fumes from the solder.
  2. Wear safety goggles to protect your eyes from hot solder and flying debris.
  3. Use heat-resistant gloves to handle hot components and avoid burns.
  4. Keep the soldering iron stand stable and away from flammable materials.
  5. Unplug the soldering iron when not in use and let it cool down before storing.
  6. Never touch the soldering iron tip directly with bare hands, as it can cause burns.
  7. Use soldering irons with temperature control features to prevent overheating.
  8. Follow manufacturer's instructions and recommended safety practices at all times.
